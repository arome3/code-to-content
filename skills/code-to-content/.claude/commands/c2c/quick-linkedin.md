# /c2c:quick-linkedin

Generate a LinkedIn post quickly with sensible defaults.

**Usage:** `/c2c:quick-linkedin [topic or insight]`

**When to use:** Fast posts from clear ideas. Skip for topics needing deep personal narrative development.

---

## Mode: QUICK (Reduced Verification)

**Skipped phases:** 1 (analysis), 2 (audience), 4 (optimization)

**Defaults applied:**
- Audience: Professional network (peers, recruiters, industry)
- Voice: Professional but authentic, not corporate
- Tone: Personal story + actionable insight

---

## Content Generation

Generate a post (800-1300 characters) with this structure:

### Hook (CRITICAL)
First 2 lines appear before "see more" â€” make them count:
- Start with a bold statement, result, or question
- Create reason to click "see more"

### Story (3-4 short paragraphs)
- Specific situation (name the project, date, context)
- Challenge you faced
- What you learned or discovered

### Takeaways (3 bullets)
- Actionable lessons others can apply
- Start each with a verb

### Engagement
- End with a question that invites responses
- Keep it open-ended, not yes/no

---

## Lite Verification

Before delivery, verify:
- [ ] Hook appears in first 2 lines
- [ ] Story is personal and specific (not generic advice)
- [ ] 3 actionable takeaways present
- [ ] Engagement question at end
- [ ] No corporate jargon

---

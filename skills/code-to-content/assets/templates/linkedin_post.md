## Character Count Guide

- Hook (visible): ~200 characters
- Full post: 1,300-3,000 characters (sweet spot: ~1,500)
- Hashtags: 3-5 relevant ones

---

## LinkedIn Best Practices

- [ ] First 2 lines are compelling (before "See more")
- [ ] Personal story, not generic advice
- [ ] Specific details (numbers, names, dates)
- [ ] Line breaks for readability
- [ ] Business/career relevance clear
- [ ] Question invites genuine discussion
- [ ] 3-5 relevant hashtags
- [ ] No external links in main text (hurts reach)
- [ ] Post link in first comment instead

---

## Content Angles for Dev/Tech

- Career growth story
- Technical decision and its impact
- Leadership/management lesson
- Failure and what you learned
- Team achievement
- Industry insight/prediction
- Hiring/interviewing experience
- Build in public update
